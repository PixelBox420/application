FAQ Section

Q: How does the collective application work?

A: Our platform enables students to apply simultaneously to Hoas and Ayy student housing. Everything is simplified you don't need to click endless boxes you check your area fill minimal details and apply! We intend to be the all in one place for student housing with Lumo student housing added next.
Simply fill out the collective application form, and we'll automatically take care of the rest!



Q: How is my personal information handled?

A: We prioritize your privacy. We do not store any user data. Your information is used exclusively for the housing application process. The page doesn't even have cookies, (unlike the hoas and ayy forms.)
